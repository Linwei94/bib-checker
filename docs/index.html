The problem

问题所在

AI writes citations.
They're often fake.

AI 在编造引用。
而你很难察觉。

Large language models confidently fabricate paper titles, author names, venues, and years. These hallucinated references pass spell-check but fail on Google Scholar — and now top venues run automated checks.

大语言模型会自信地编造论文标题、作者、会议名和年份。这些幻觉引用通过拼写检查，但在 Google Scholar 上根本找不到——而 ICML、ICLR 等顶会已经开始自动核查参考文献。

🤖

LLMs hallucinate references

LLM 会编造参考文献

ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini — all generate plausible-looking but nonexistent citations. The more obscure the topic, the worse it gets.

ChatGPT、Claude、Gemini 都会生成看起来合理但根本不存在的引用。主题越小众，幻觉越严重。

@article{zhang2023neural,
  title={Neural Scaling Laws for...},
  author={Zhang, Wei and Liu, Fang},
  journal={Nature Machine Intelligence}
}
🔍

Top venues now check

顶会已开始自动核查

ICML, ICLR, and NeurIPS have begun flagging papers with unverifiable references. A single hallucinated citation can trigger a desk rejection or integrity review.

ICML、ICLR、NeurIPS 已开始标记含有无法核实引用的论文。一条幻觉引用就可能导致 desk rejection 或学术诚信审查。

⏱️

Manual checking takes hours

手动核查费时费力

Verifying 50+ references one by one on Google Scholar is tedious and error-prone. Most researchers skip it — until it's too late.

逐条在 Google Scholar 核查 50+ 条引用既繁琐又容易出错。大多数研究者直接跳过——直到出事才后悔。

📋

Wrong metadata hurts too

错误元数据同样有害

Even real papers can have outdated venue names, wrong years, or missing DOIs in AI-generated BibTeX. DeHallucinate catches metadata errors too.

即使是真实论文，AI 生成的 BibTeX 也可能有过时的会议名、错误年份或缺失 DOI。DeHallucinate 同样能检测元数据错误。

How it works

工作原理

Three steps to clean references

三步完成全量引用核查

📋
1

Paste your .bib file

粘贴 .bib 文件

Paste your BibTeX content into the left panel. DeHallucinate parses all entries automatically.

将 BibTeX 内容粘贴到左侧面板，DeHallucinate 自动解析所有条目。

🤖
2

Batch-fetch from Scholar

批量从 Scholar 获取

One click opens Google Scholar for each entry, automatically clicks Cite → BibTeX, and fetches the real metadata — no manual work.

一键自动为每条引用打开 Google Scholar、点击 Cite → BibTeX，获取真实元数据——全程无需手动操作。

✅
3

Review the diff & fix

查看差异并修正

A field-by-field diff with word-level highlights shows exactly what's wrong. Replace individually or hit "Replace All" in one click.

逐字段差异对比（词级高亮）清楚展示哪里有问题。可逐条替换，也可一键"全部替换"。

Features

核心功能

Everything you need,
nothing you don't

简单直接，够用就好

⚡

Batch fetch, fully automated

全自动批量获取

Opens Scholar tabs, clicks Cite → BibTeX, closes them — all automatically. Just watch it go.

自动打开 Scholar 标签页、点击 Cite → BibTeX、关闭标签页，全程自动。

🔬

Word-level diff highlights

词级差异高亮

Changed words are highlighted in amber/green. Case differences are ignored so you only see meaningful changes.

改动词语用橙/绿高亮标出，大小写差异自动忽略，只显示真正的差异。

🔑

Keep your citation keys

保留原始 key

Replace metadata while preserving your original BibTeX keys — no broken \cite{} references in your paper.

替换元数据时保留原始 key，论文中的 \cite{} 引用不受影响。

🎯

Replace All in one click

一键全部替换

After reviewing, apply all Scholar-verified BibTeX entries to your file at once. Or replace individually.

审阅完成后，一键将所有 Scholar 核实的条目批量写入文件，也可逐条替换。

↩

Undo anytime

随时撤销

Full undo history. Made a wrong replacement? One keystroke takes you back.

完整撤销历史，替换错了按 Ctrl+Z 立刻恢复。

🔒

100% local — no data sent

完全本地，不上传数据

Your BibTeX never leaves your browser. The only external requests are to Google Scholar — the same ones you'd make manually.

BibTeX 内容始终在本地，唯一的外部请求是访问 Google Scholar——和你手动操作完全一样。
